Energy Saver:
Description:
Energy Saver is an app where you can find the best and important and most useful methods to save energy. A complete and precise guide about saving energy, specially based on Power Consumption. 

Features:
•	Fully research based application to Saving Energy.
•	The Research results on How to save energy by applying most useful techniques.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
•	Refresh Every Page of App.
